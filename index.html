Asked beforeevery demo

Is it really handwritten?

Cards are written in real pen-on-paper handwriting style — not a printed font pretending. Letters are typeset like fine stationery and signed in handwriting style. Your clients receive mail that looks and feels personal, because it is.

What's in a Local History or Heritage letter?

Local History letters tell the story of the recipient's own street, neighborhood, and the year their house was built. Heritage letters go personal: their family name's story, their hometown, the world the year they were born — all driven by a 2-minute profile you fill out once per recipient.

What do I actually have to do?

One thing, once: send us your past-client list (names, addresses, closing dates). After that, nothing. Mail goes out on schedule, signed from you, and you get a monthly recap of every piece sent.

Can I see a sample before I commit?

Yes — every plan is month to month, so your first month is the sample. Or email us before you order and we'll mail one piece to your own door first.

Does this work for car dealerships?

It's built for you too. Purchase-anniversary letters, service-season cards, birthdays — signed from the salesperson who closed the deal. Your buyers bring the trade-in back to the store that remembered them.

Can I send to my own family and friends?

Yes — that's the For Families plans. Build your list, give each person a profile, pick their categories, and we mail 1, 3, or 10 pieces a month signed from you. Preview, pause, or swap anyone at any time. You control all of it.

Is there a contract?

No. Every plan is month to month, and you can cancel anytime. We'd rather earn the renewal with the mail than with a clause.
