The things peopleask first

Is it really handwritten?

Yes — every card is written in real pen-on-paper handwriting style, not a printed font pretending to be one. It looks and feels like you sat down and wrote it, because that's the whole point of a card.

Do I have to write the message myself?

You can write exactly what you want, or pick from warm, ready-made messages and make them yours. Either way it goes out signed in your name — never ours.

Will the people I send to know I used a service?

No. There's no AfterSold branding on the card or envelope. As far as your grandma knows, you remembered her birthday and sat down to write — which, in every way that counts, you did.

What if I want to change a card before it goes out?

We send you a reminder before each card mails, so you can update the message, add news, or skip it. Nothing goes out behind your back.

Is there a contract?

None. Monthly plans are month to month and the Year Plan is a one-time setup — cancel anytime. We'd rather keep you with good cards than with fine print.

I run a business — can AfterSold do that too?

Yes. AfterSold for Business mails anniversary, birthday, and local-history letters to every client a real estate agent or car dealer has ever closed — so the repeat call comes back to you.
