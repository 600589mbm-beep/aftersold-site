Asked beforeevery demo

Is it really handwritten?

Every card is written in real pen-on-paper handwriting style — not a printed font pretending. Your clients receive a card that looks and feels like you sat down and wrote it. That's the entire point.

What do I actually have to do?

One thing, once: send us your past-client list (names, addresses, closing dates). After that, nothing. Cards go out on schedule, signed from you, and you get a monthly recap of every card sent.

Can I see a sample before I commit?

Yes — that's what the demo is for. Book the 10-minute call and we'll mail a free sample card to your own door, so you can judge exactly what your clients would hold in their hands.

What if I have more than 125 clients?

Tell us on the demo call. We quote larger lists individually so you're not paying a one-size-fits-none rate for a database you've spent a career building.

Is there a contract?

No. Both plans are month to month, and you can cancel anytime. We'd rather earn the renewal with the cards than with a clause.
